Client Discovery Tool

Uncover the North Star Values that drive every decision

Use this tool during client interviews to capture responses, track emotional patterns, and identify the core values driving your client's audience. Everything saves locally in your browser — no data leaves your machine.

💡 Interview Best Practices

  • Have conversations when people feel comfortable and open
  • Listen for patterns across different answers — don't rush to categorize
  • Pay attention to emotional responses and what they reject as much as what they embrace
  • Notice inconsistencies between claimed and actual values — actions reveal more than words
  • Use the observation tags below each question to mark what you notice in the moment

Values Interpretation

Based on the interview, identify which North Star values are driving this person. Select all that apply, then rate their strength. Look for repeated themes, strong emotions, and consistent patterns.

🔍 Strongest Indicators Come From

  • What they criticize in others (reveals their own standards)
  • Where they won't compromise (reveals non-negotiable values)
  • Who they admire and why (reveals aspirational values)
  • What they want to be known for (reveals identity values)
