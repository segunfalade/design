Client Discovery Interview

Strategic positioning for coaches, consultants, and course creators targeting midlifers & skeptics.

Strategist Note: Do not mindlessly read these questions. Weave them into natural conversation. Listen for patterns across answers. Notice what they reject as much as what they embrace.

1. Past Decisions & Patterns

Listen for: What made it significant? How did they know it was right? What would others have done?

Listen for: What makes it important? Always true? How has it guided choices?

2. Success & Status

Listen for: How would they feel? What would others notice? What changes daily?

3. Life Approach

Listen for: Why do others act that way? How do they do it differently? What do others not understand?

4. Future & Legacy

Listen for: Who would recognize this? Why does it matter? Steps taken?

5. Hidden Motivators

Listen for: Why doesn't it appeal? What are others really after? What pursue instead?

Listen for: How did they conclude this? What do others miss? How does it influence choices?

North Star Diagnosis

Based on your notes, which values drive this client? (Select primary ones to build your strategy)

Strongest Indicators To Look For

  • What they criticize in others
  • Where they won't compromise
  • Who they admire and why
  • What they want to be known for

Pitfalls to Avoid

  • Don't stop at surface-level (ask "why" and "how")
  • Don't rush to categorize (let patterns emerge)
